Todo Liverpool en un QR

Extension of the Liverpool App that uses product's QR codes to enhance clients experience inside the stores.

  • Scan a product QR code to get available sizes, prices and discounts in the store.
  • Scan a store QR code to get which products from your wish list are available in that store.
  • Each time you scan a QR code you receive points that can be used in purchases.
  • When you add a clothe to your wish list or scan it, the app shows you other clothes and accesories that match with that clothe in different outfits.

Tools: JavaScript, Firebase, HTMl, Sass, Bootstrap

We Want iOS app

We Want to promote social change. This iOS app allows users to report problems and issues that public spaces in Mexico City have. Our app encourages users to make reports with our points program.

  • See reports made by the community in the home map.
  • Upload a photo to describe better the problem and our Machine Learning model will identify a category for the issue so it can be grouped with other reports.
  • When you upload the report an algorithm will identify the most important words in it so it can be highlighted in the map and improve the report searching.
  • Share your user code with your friends and make reports to add points to your account.
  • See which stores have special coupons and exchange them for your points.

Tools: Swift, Swift Storyboard, Firebase, Create ML, Microsoft Text Analytics API
